Welcome to Rooted Prints!

My name is Lexie Star. I have been addicted to photography since 2018, so I am excited to be sharing my photos through Rooted Prints and honored that they will be decorating the walls of many homes. Some of my favorite things that you will find here are photos of native plants, natural landscapes, and urban details in Minnesota and around the world. Thank you for supporting my art!
